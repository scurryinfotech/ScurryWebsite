About Scurry Infotech

Early Traction Stage Startup - We've built a working prototype of our revolutionary web app that allows users to book specific tables at restaurants and pre-order food for a seamless dining experience.

Our innovative platform addresses the significant pain point of long restaurant queues and inefficient table management by introducing real-time booking with AI-based wait-time estimation and pre-ordering capabilities.

Unlike other food platforms focused on delivery, we exclusively enhance the dine-in experience, creating value for both customers and restaurant owners.
